Given $X_1 ∼ \mathcal{N}(µ_1, σ_1^2)$ and $X_2 ∼ \mathcal{N}(µ_2, σ_2^2)$:
	If $X_1$ and $X_2$ are independent, then:
	$$ X_1 + X_2 ∼ \mathcal{N}(µ_1 + µ_2, σ_1^2 + σ_2^2) $$
