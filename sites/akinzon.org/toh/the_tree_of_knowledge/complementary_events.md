The complementary event of $E$ in $\Omega$, $E^C$ is defined as:
$$ E^C = \Omega\ \backslash \ E $$
Therefore, 
$$ \mathbb{P}(E^C) = 1\ -\ \mathbb{P}(E) $$

The complement of $E$ is useful when counting $E$ itself is tedious.
