Variance is calculated by:
$$ \mathbb{V}[X] = \mathbb{E} [ (X − \mathbb{E}[X])^2 ]$$

Variance = $standard\ deviation^2$


It can also be calculated by:
$$ \mathbb{V}[X] = \mathbb{E}[X^2] − (\mathbb{E}[X])^2 $$
