A partition of a set $X$ is a collection $X_1, ..., X_n$ where:
$$ X_1\ \cup\ ...\ \cup\ X_n = X $$
And
$$ \forall 1 \le i,j \le n,\ i \ne j $$
implying that 
$$ X_i\ \cap X_j = \emptyset $$
