### Blu-Ray

Uses 405nm blue laser, with a typical size of 15-30GiB (some can be 128GiB)

Decent for offline storage, but terribly slow (70MiB/s)

### Magnetic tape

SUPER large - 12TB to 36TB

Good for backups

**SUPER SLOW** - Its speeds are quoted in GB/hour
