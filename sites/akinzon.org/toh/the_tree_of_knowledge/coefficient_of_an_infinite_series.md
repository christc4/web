> [!TIP]- What is $[x^{22}](x + x^2 + · · ·)^3$? 
> This is the same as $[x^{19}](1 + x + · · ·)^3$, use the coefficient formula accordingly: 
> $\binom{19 + 3 - 1}{19}=\binom{21}{19}=210$

looks similar to $\binom{n}{r}$ with repetition - see [Combinations](combinations)
